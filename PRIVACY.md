# Privacy Policy

**HearZork** is an open source Z-machine interpreter for iOS and macOS, designed for visually impaired players.

## Data Collection

This app does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

## Network Usage

The app connects to GitHub to fetch a catalog of downloadable games and to download game files at your request. No user data is sent during these connections.

## Microphone and Speech

HearZork uses the device microphone for voice commands via Apple's on-device speech recognition. Audio is processed locally on your device and is **not** sent to any server. Speech recognition requires your explicit permission and can be disabled at any time.

## Local Storage

All data (game files, settings) is stored locally on your device in the app's private container. This data is not accessible to other apps and is not transmitted anywhere.

## Analytics

This app does not include any analytics, tracking, or advertising frameworks.

## Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party services that collect user data.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue at:
https://github.com/avwohl/hearzork/issues

## Changes

This privacy policy may be updated occasionally. Changes will be posted to this repository.
